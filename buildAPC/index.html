Step 7

  1. Mount the Cooling Fan
  2. The fan plug is at the TOP of the motherboard

Step 8: The Case

  1. Screw in the stand offs into the case
  2. They will match up with the holes on the motherboard that have metal rings on them.

Step 8.5: Note

I had some issues finding the correct screws when I was origionally building this, and I missed putting in the motherboard cover.  Be sure you place it in the slot before you screw in the motherboard.

Step 9: Plug it up

  1. Plug in the 8 pin and the 24pin cabes from the power supply to the motherboard
  2. Plug the case fan into the socket labled case fan right above the 4 SATA 2.0 sockets (they are blue).
  3. Plug in the SATA cables from the SSD and the DVD into the two SATA 3.0 sockets (they are white)
  4. Time to get to the really annoying part, the front panels. Find the plugs labed reset, power and HDD LED
    1. Plug them in to the correct pins at the bottom of the motherboard
    2. All of their labels should face up.
  5. Look for the audio connector and plug it into the pins for the audio connectors
  6. Look for the USB 2.0 and plug it into the bottom USB 2.0 pins, be sure to match up the blank pin with the blank pin to assure they match up with the correct wires

Step 9: Plug it up
